About Apalara Yoruba

We teach the Yoruba language and culture through live classes, cultural activities, and a structured year-long curriculum.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To preserve and spread Yorùbá culture worldwide by making learning fun, interactive, and accessible for everyone.

Vision: A global community confident in speaking Yoruba and proud of their cultural heritage.

How Did We Start…

I started teaching Yoruba out of a passion to share the language and culture I grew up loving. Inspired by my grandmother, who always reminded me that “our language is the key to our roots,” I realized how important it is to help others connect with their heritage. Today, I guide learners to speak Yoruba confidently while exploring the richness of its traditions and stories. Teaching Yoruba is more than lessons it’s about keeping our culture alive and passing it on to the next generation.

We are dedicated to fostering the use of the Yoruba language by teaching it through the use of innovative tools and encouraging people of all ages around the world to engage with and communicate in Yoruba language while upholding the cultural values.
