Meet Our CEO

Toluwalope Kodaolu is a passionate Yoruba Language Instructor with a B.A. and Master’s Degree in Yoruba Linguistics from the University of Ibadan. She also holds a Postgraduate Diploma in Organization Management from Cambrian College, Canada, where she is currently studying Early Childhood Education

Toluwalope teaches Yoruba Language at St. Albert Adult Learning Centre in Ontario, Canada, and is the Founder of Apalara Yoruba Language School , a platform helping children and adults in the diaspora learn to communicate confidently in Yoruba. She is committed to preserving the Yoruba language and believes that language is identity, and identity must not be lost.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To preserve and spread Yorùbá culture worldwide by making learning fun, interactive, and accessible for everyone.

Vision: To preserve the Yoruba language, pass on cultural heritage, and raise confident communicators who are proud of their identity.
