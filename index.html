Learn Yoruba: Speak. Connect. Preserve.

Connect with Yoruba language and culture from anywhere, starting at $0. We bring communities across the US, Canada, UK, and Europe together through innovative learning tools.

A ń gbé èdè Yorùbá ga nípasẹ̀ ẹ̀kọ́ pẹ̀lú àwọn irinṣẹ́ tuntun, tí a sì ń ràn gbogbo ènìyàn lórílẹ̀-àyé létí láti bá a ṣe sọ̀rọ̀ ní Yorùbá àti láti ní ìmọ̀ nípa àṣà wa

We are dedicated to fostering the use of the Yoruba language by teaching it through the use of innovative tools and encouraging people of all ages around the world to engage with and communicate in Yoruba language while upholding the cultural values.

Learning a new language can be exciting but also challenging, especially if you’re not in an environment where it’s spoken regularly. Whether you’re a complete beginner with no knowledge of Yoruba, have some understanding but struggle to speak confidently, or somewhere in between looking to improve your skills, this journey is designed to help you grow.

Our Classes

Intro Yoruba

Starting a new language can feel overwhelming, but we’re here to build your confidence and guide you every step of the way toward becoming a fluent Yoruba speaker.

Beginners Yoruba

You may already have a basic understanding of the Yoruba language. Our intermediate course will help you deepen that knowledge and master how to confidently apply the core concepts of the language.

Intermediate Yoruba

Want to deepen your Yoruba skills beyond simple conversations? Interested in learning key grammar concepts and connecting more deeply with Yoruba culture? Let us walk with you on this meaningful learning journey

Advanced Yoruba

Are you ready to move beyond basic conversation and truly master the Yoruba language? Join us as we explore the intricacies of Yoruba grammar, delve into its nuanced expressions, and immerse ourselves in the depth of Yoruba culture.
