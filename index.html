We are dedicated to fostering the use of the Yorùbá language by teaching it through the use of innovative tools and encouraging people of all ages around the world to engage with and communicate in Yorùbá language while upholding the cultural values.

Learning a new language can be exciting but also challenging, especially if you’re not in an environment where it’s spoken regularly. Whether you’re a complete beginner with no knowledge of Yorùbá, have some understanding but struggle to speak confidently, or somewhere in between looking to improve your skills, this journey is designed to help you grow.

Our Classes

Beginners

Are you just starting your Yorùbá journey? Our Beginners Class is perfect for absolute beginners with no prior knowledge of the language. Through this course, you will:

  • Learn to read and write short, simple texts
  • Communicate in everyday situations
  • Build a solid foundation for continued learning.

Intermediate

If you already know the basics of Yorùbá, our Intermediate Class will help you take your skills further. You will:

  • Develop a stronger understanding of grammar.
  • Read and write moderately complex texts
  • Engage in meaningful conversations with confidence

Advanced

Ready to communicate fluently? The Advanced Class focuses on mastery of more complex structures and real-life application. You will:

  • Express ideas clearly in both spoken and written Yorùbá
  • Understand longer and more complex texts.
  • Participate confidently in conversations with native speakers.
  • Apply Yorùbá in a variety of real-world contexts

Mastery

For those who want to *become truly fluent, our Mastery Class takes your skills to an expert level. You will:

  • Achieve *near-native fluency* in reading, writing, and speaking
  • Understand and use *sophisticated expressions and sentence structures
  • Engage with authentic Yorùbá texts and materials
